Breathe life: Cleaning the air, saving lives, and slowing near-term global warming

Air pollution causes 7 million premature deaths every year (WHO). A new WHO/CCAC Report, 'Reducing global health risks through mitigation of short-lived climate pollutants' (WHO, 2015) highlights the broad opportunities for better health available through reducing emissions of short-lived climate pollutants, which contribute significantly both to air pollution as well as to climate change.
